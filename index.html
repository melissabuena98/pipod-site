Turn your dreams into reality

Neumont's Innovation Lab connects students to resources beyond campus to help them turn their projects into businesses. These resources provide students with the tools they need to build prototypes of their ideas, test their projects, and gives them access to funding groups. Students will have the opportunity to create a business model for their idea and work with industry experts to develop their idea. Your ideas can change the world, let the Innovation Lab show you how.

IT'S YOURS TO KEEP

Your projects at Neumont are yours to keep. That's right, you retain all intellectual property rights to your ideas and projects. As an institution, we believe we're here to help you reach your full potential. We'll help you turn your ideas and projects into a business through the Innovation Lab and our partnerships in the community.

MAKE YOUR PROJECT Your Business

You develop remarkable projects throughout your education at Neumont. Why not make it into a viable business?

The Innovation Lab assists students in turning their projects into businesses. The Lab connects student teams with other entrepreneurs across the state through meaningful partnerships in business, technology, and government. With the Lab's help, you can turn your idea into reality.

INNOVATION LAB PARTNERS:

Student teams participating in the Innovation lab will have access to the resources listed below. These partner organizations are committed to helping Neumont students find expertise outside the computer science field like medical, engineering, accounting and more to help them succeed.

Partners

Join the Innovation Lab

Students and alumni looking to join the Innovation Lab must submit an application. Applicants will be judged on the project's potential for success in the marketplace. Additionally, priority will be given to projects that work across degrees and communities.
